NDAA §848 Spec Sheet Viewer

Searchable index of public component-origin compliance documents from UAS manufacturers. The first free cross-vendor aggregator — no such database exists at DoD, DIU, or SAM.gov.

NDAA §848 (FY2020) + ASDA / FAR 52.240-1 (FY2024, effective Dec 22, 2025) + FCC Covered List (Dec 22, 2025) together form the compliance stack that every US UAS buyer must now navigate. Vendors publish their own self-attested PDFs — there is no DoD-issued form. This page links directly to each vendor's source document with no re-hosting.
FCC Covered List update, Dec 22 2025: The FCC has added all foreign-produced UAS and critical UAS components to the Covered List, making it illegal to authorize them for US use — regardless of who the buyer is. DJI and Autel are named explicitly. Blue UAS / Blue UAS Framework items are narrowly exempt. This affects every commercial US buyer, not just federal procurement. FCC DA-25-1086 →

About this index. All documents linked here are self-attested by their manufacturers unless marked as Blue UAS or Blue UAS Framework (which adds DoD cybersecurity and hardware review). There is no government-issued §848 spec sheet template — each vendor rolls their own. Forge does not re-host PDFs; every link points to the vendor's original. Missing data (null values) means the vendor has not publicly addressed that statute, not that they are non-compliant. Dates reflect the attestation date on the source document, not today's status.
