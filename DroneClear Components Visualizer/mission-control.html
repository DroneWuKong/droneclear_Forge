About Forge

Forge is the interactive build planner for the Drone Integration Handbook — making the handbook's data tactile for different learners. Browse vetted parts, validate build compatibility in real-time, follow step-by-step assembly guides, and maintain full audit trails. Built on DroneClear by Ted Strazimiri — 3,000+ real components across 12 categories, with a compatibility engine that checks mounting patterns, voltage ranges, connector types, and more.
