SpaceDrone HD 5"

By helldiver on Apr 13, 2026

1  26  0

https://spacedrones.sk/5-inch-hd/#sample

Beefed-up 5inch spacedrone (8mm pulwinded tubular arms, instead of 7mm pulltruded), to better cope with 2040kv RCinpower GTS V4 motors, now filtering could be properly decreased for super quick reactions.
Hit 242km/h with 3,6 pitch triblades, even more with 4,3 pitch props.

Total build weight without battery is 271g, 475g with 1300mah 6S Tatu R-line V6 li-po.

Short speed testing video -> https://spacedrones.sk/5_inch_hd_pyramid_drone/video/speed_test.mp4
